Who We Are?

Globy is the first professional company to assist youth, especially Indonesian, in achieving international achievement, particularly in Debate, Model United Nations (MUN) & Public Speaking.

Our journey began on September 1st, 2020, solidified by legal recognition via the Decree of the Minister of Law and Human Rights of the Republic of Indonesia, No. AHU-0234691.AHU.01.11. We proudly operate under the legal entity PT. Globy Lentera Nusantara.

How do we Collaborate?

Competition Mentoring & Coaching

We provide schools with the capability for their international programs, especially in Model United Nations (MUN) and Debate. Moreover, we build tailor-made curricula that are adapted to the needs and capabilities of students.

English Club & MUN Club Mentoring

We nurture students through weekly meetings in their English Club and/or MUN Club. We create a fun learning environment for students to empower them for international competitions.

MUN & Conference Event Organizer

We assist institutions in building MUN and/or international conference events. Our services include chair (for MUN), academic assistance, event consultation, promotion, and customer service.
